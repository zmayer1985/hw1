Zach Mayer's Top 4 travel destinations

I've visited 29 countries and here are my top 4 travel destinations

  • 1. Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
  • 2. Roatan, Honduras
  • 3. Buenos Aires, Argentina
  • 4. Vail, Colorado

    • 1. Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

    What's so great about Rio?

    Rio de Janeiro, the 2nd largest city in Brazil, is home to a great mix of beaches, urban conveniences, and culture that is unrivaled. While the city is nearly 20mm people, the tourist area is only a few square blocks, making Rio a very easy city to learn and navigate.

    2. Roatan, Honduras

    What's so great about Roatan?

    Roatan, an island off the coast of Honduras, sports some of the best unspoiled beaches in the world and is a popular cruise destination for U.S. cruiseliners.

    3. Buenos Aires, Argentina

    What's so great about Buenos Aires?

    Buenos Aires lives up to its reputation as the "Paris of South America" with historical architecture coupled with a vibrant culture, nightlife, and cuisine.

    4. Vail, Colorado

    What's so great about Vail?

    Vail is one of the premier winter ski destinations in the U.S. and offers an adventerous mix of advanced terrain, beginner slopes, shopping, food, and drink.
